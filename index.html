How it works

Our expectimax-powered AI thinks several moves ahead to keep the board alive and chase the legendary 2048 tile.

Game plan

Spin up the AI or take the wheel yourself. Stack combos, ride the chain reactions, and push the score multiplier sky high.

Controls: Use your arrow keys to slide every tile. Combine equal tiles to level up the board.

Behind the scenes: This clone ships with an expectimax AI that evaluates thousands of boards per second to keep the combos flowing.
